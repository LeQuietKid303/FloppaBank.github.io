Different Currencies

I think this is pretty obvious, but every country has their own Currency, such as USD, EUR, JPY, and many more!
Now, Currencies are a huge part in our lives, and for granted, jobs. We use Currencies to know how much money we have,
whether that's in our Currency or another Currency, which can be helpful for traveling, or a guide or possibly even tourist.
Different places use different terms depending on an item in their Currency, for example, Canada uses the term Toonie for a coin
that's worth 2 Canadian Dollars, and a Loonie for 1 Canadian Dollar.
